What is HyperText

Back

HyperText is text which is not constrained to be linear.
HyperText is text which conatains links to other texts. The term was coined by Ted Nelson around 1965 (see History)
HyperMedia is a term used for hypertext which is not constrained to be text: it can include graphics, Video and Sound, for example.
Apparently Ted Nelson was the first to used this term too.

Hypertext and hyperMedia are concepts, not Products.
